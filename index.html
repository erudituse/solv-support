About

It started with a train I almost missed.

15 seconds of running—that's all it took before I was gasping for air. Hands on knees, I knew something had to change.

So I started training. But being lazy by nature, I didn't want to waste effort on things that weren't working. I needed to know: "Is what I'm doing actually making a difference?"

That question became Solv—a training intelligence app that doesn't just show you what happened, but what it means.
